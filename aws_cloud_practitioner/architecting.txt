what is security in the well-architect pillars?: protect systems while delivering business value
what is reliability in the well-architect pillars?: systems ability to recover from disruptions
what is performance efficiency in the well-architect pillars?: use compute resources efficiently
what is cost optimization in the well-architect pillars?: optimize costs
what is sustainability in the well-architect pillars?: reduce the amount of energy

reliability design principles: simulate failures, automatically recover from failure

cost optimization desing principles: migrate to cloud, use tags on resources, use manage services
